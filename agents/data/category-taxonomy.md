# HR Ticket Category Taxonomy

## 1. Absence Management

### 1.1 Sick Leave
- **Definition**: Employee reports illness or injury preventing work attendance
- **Required Information**: Medical certificate (if absence > 3 days), start date, expected return date
- **Operator Group**: HR-Absence-Management

### 1.2 Vacation Request
- **Definition**: Employee requests planned time off (annual leave, compensatory days)
- **Required Information**: Start date, end date, manager approval status
- **Operator Group**: HR-Absence-Management

### 1.3 Maternity/Paternity Leave
- **Definition**: Employee requests parental leave due to birth or adoption
- **Required Information**: Expected delivery/adoption date, desired leave start date, confirmation letter from doctor/agency
- **Operator Group**: HR-Absence-Management

### 1.4 Unpaid Leave
- **Definition**: Employee requests leave without pay for personal reasons
- **Required Information**: Start date, end date, reason, manager approval
- **Operator Group**: HR-Absence-Management

### 1.5 Bereavement Leave
- **Definition**: Employee requests time off due to death of a family member
- **Required Information**: Relationship to deceased, death certificate (for extended leave > 3 days)
- **Operator Group**: HR-Absence-Management

## 2. Personnel Administration

### 2.1 Address Change
- **Definition**: Employee reports a change of residential address
- **Required Information**: New street address, postal code, city, canton/state, move date
- **Operator Group**: HR-Personnel-Admin

### 2.2 Name Change
- **Definition**: Employee reports a legal name change (marriage, divorce, court order)
- **Required Information**: New legal name, official document (marriage certificate, court order, ID)
- **Operator Group**: HR-Personnel-Admin

### 2.3 Bank Details Change
- **Definition**: Employee requests update to salary payment account
- **Required Information**: New IBAN, bank name, account holder name confirmation
- **Operator Group**: HR-Payroll

### 2.4 Civil Status Change
- **Definition**: Employee reports change in marital/civil status
- **Required Information**: New status (married, divorced, widowed, registered partnership), official document
- **Operator Group**: HR-Personnel-Admin

### 2.5 Emergency Contact Update
- **Definition**: Employee updates their emergency contact information
- **Required Information**: Contact name, relationship, phone number
- **Operator Group**: HR-Personnel-Admin

## 3. Payroll & Compensation

### 3.1 Salary Question
- **Definition**: Employee has a question about their pay slip or salary calculation
- **Required Information**: Pay period in question, specific line item or amount questioned
- **Operator Group**: HR-Payroll

### 3.2 Expense Reimbursement
- **Definition**: Employee submits expenses for reimbursement
- **Required Information**: Receipts, expense type, business justification, amount, currency
- **Operator Group**: HR-Payroll

### 3.3 Tax Certificate Request
- **Definition**: Employee requests annual tax certificate or wage statement
- **Required Information**: Tax year, delivery preference (digital/postal)
- **Operator Group**: HR-Payroll

### 3.4 Bonus/Variable Pay Query
- **Definition**: Employee inquires about bonus, commission, or variable compensation
- **Required Information**: Compensation period, specific plan reference
- **Operator Group**: HR-Payroll

## 4. Benefits & Insurance

### 4.1 Health Insurance Query
- **Definition**: Employee asks about employer health insurance coverage
- **Required Information**: Nature of query (enrollment, coverage, claims)
- **Operator Group**: HR-Benefits

### 4.2 Pension/Retirement Query
- **Definition**: Employee asks about pension contributions or retirement planning
- **Required Information**: Nature of query, retirement timeline (if applicable)
- **Operator Group**: HR-Benefits

### 4.3 Family Allowance
- **Definition**: Employee requests child/family allowance or reports change in dependents
- **Required Information**: Child's name, date of birth, birth certificate (for new child)
- **Operator Group**: HR-Benefits

### 4.4 Insurance Claim
- **Definition**: Employee reports a workplace accident or submits insurance claim
- **Required Information**: Date of incident, description, medical report (if applicable)
- **Operator Group**: HR-Benefits

## 5. Employment Lifecycle

### 5.1 Onboarding Query
- **Definition**: New hire has questions about start date, documents, or first-day logistics
- **Required Information**: Employee ID or offer letter reference, specific question
- **Operator Group**: HR-Onboarding

### 5.2 Resignation
- **Definition**: Employee submits notice of resignation
- **Required Information**: Intended last working day, written resignation letter
- **Operator Group**: HR-Offboarding

### 5.3 Reference Letter Request
- **Definition**: Employee (current or former) requests a work reference/employment letter
- **Required Information**: Purpose (new employer, visa, other), preferred language
- **Operator Group**: HR-Personnel-Admin

### 5.4 Contract Question
- **Definition**: Employee has questions about their employment contract terms
- **Required Information**: Specific clause or topic of inquiry
- **Operator Group**: HR-Personnel-Admin

### 5.5 Internal Transfer
- **Definition**: Employee requests or inquires about internal transfer/mobility
- **Required Information**: Target department/role, current manager awareness
- **Operator Group**: HR-Talent-Management

## 6. Training & Development

### 6.1 Training Request
- **Definition**: Employee requests enrollment in a training course or certification
- **Required Information**: Course name, provider, dates, cost, manager approval
- **Operator Group**: HR-Learning-Development

### 6.2 Education Reimbursement
- **Definition**: Employee requests reimbursement for education expenses
- **Required Information**: Program name, institution, receipts, completion proof
- **Operator Group**: HR-Learning-Development

### 6.3 Performance Review Query
- **Definition**: Employee has questions about the performance review process
- **Required Information**: Review period, specific concern
- **Operator Group**: HR-Talent-Management

## 7. Workplace & Compliance

### 7.1 Harassment/Discrimination Report
- **Definition**: Employee reports workplace harassment or discrimination
- **Required Information**: Description of incident, date, parties involved (handled confidentially)
- **Operator Group**: HR-Compliance
- **Priority**: HIGH — escalate immediately

### 7.2 Work-from-Home Request
- **Definition**: Employee requests remote work arrangement
- **Required Information**: Proposed schedule, duration (temporary/permanent), justification
- **Operator Group**: HR-Personnel-Admin

### 7.3 Workplace Accommodation
- **Definition**: Employee requests accommodation due to disability or health condition
- **Required Information**: Nature of accommodation needed, medical documentation
- **Operator Group**: HR-Personnel-Admin

### 7.4 Whistleblower Report
- **Definition**: Employee reports compliance violation or unethical behavior
- **Required Information**: Description of concern, evidence if available (handled confidentially)
- **Operator Group**: HR-Compliance
- **Priority**: HIGH — escalate immediately
